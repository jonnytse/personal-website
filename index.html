ABOUT ME

My name is Jonathan Tse - pronounced 'Chay' - and I am a DevOps-focused junior software developer. I completed a 12-week immersive coding bootcamp with _nology.io between January-April 2020, where I learnt the skills to become developer-ready in HTML, (S)CSS, JavaScript, React, Firebase, APIs and databases.


In my previous life, I worked at investment banks for 17 years selling Emerging Markets and Foreign Exchange products to hedge funds; I embarked on this exciting career change into software development because I am driven to learn new things and want to retain workplace relevance, whilst embracing technological advances within the global economy.


With my particular career experience, I bring an analytical and macro-minded approach to problem solving, which is greatly reinforced by my strong interpersonal skills within the stakeholder space. I am keen to commence the second phase of my career, and this galvanises me to establish myself quickly within the DevOps universe.
